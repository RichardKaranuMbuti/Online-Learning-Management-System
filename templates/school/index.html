Features

We offer a variety of features here at OLMS

Course Management: Simplify course creation, material uploading, and student progress monitoring. Easily organize and deliver course content.

User Management: Provide secure user registration, authentication, and access control for students, instructors, and administrators.

Content Management: Upload and organize various course materials, making them easily accessible to students.

Communication Tools: Include discussion forums, chat rooms, and messaging systems to facilitate interaction and collaboration.

Assessment and Grading: Streamline assessment creation, grading, and feedback, allowing instructors to track student performance.

Progress Tracking: Offer tools to monitor student attendance, assignment completion, and scores.
